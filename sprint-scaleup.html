Scale-up
Review

€2,000 flat project fee  ·  quoted per scope

An independent technical review to identify scale-up risks, equipment gaps, and process limitations before you commit to pilot investment.

What's included

  • Review of your lab or pilot-scale data and documented assumptions
  • Material-to-system performance gap analysis
  • Process and equipment specification review
  • Heat and mass-transfer limitation identification
  • Scale-up risk register — each risk ranked low / medium / high
  • Equipment sizing recommendations for the pilot stage
  • 20–30 page written review report including:
    • Summary of scale-up readiness
    • Gap analysis table: lab vs. pilot performance
    • Risk register with mitigation recommendations
    • Equipment and process recommendations
    • Suggested pilot design parameters
  • 60-min workshop with your team to discuss findings and next steps

Good for you if

  • You've proven your concept in the lab and are planning a pilot plant
  • You are designing or evaluating pilot-scale equipment options
  • You want an independent check on your scale-up assumptions before committing budget
  • You need to identify and rank technical risks for investors or management
  • You are preparing for technology transfer from research to production

What you'll walk away with

A clear scale-up readiness picture

Know exactly what will and won't transfer from lab to pilot scale.

A ranked risk register

Prioritised list of scale-up challenges so you tackle the critical ones first.

Concrete equipment recommendations

Specific guidance on reactor sizing, heat exchange, instrumentation, and process parameters for your pilot.

Typical questions we answer

"Our material works at gram scale — what are the risks at kilogram scale?"
"We've chosen a reactor type — is it the right choice for pilot scale?"
"What heat exchanger capacity do we need to maintain the same conversion rate?"
"Which assumptions from our lab setup will break down at pilot scale?"
