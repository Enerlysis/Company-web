The problem

R&D teams lose time when materials, simulation and process design are disconnected.

Deep-tech teams often have promising lab results but struggle with reactor geometry, thermal gradients, mass-transfer limitations, material degradation, uncertain boundary conditions and scale-up risk.

Enerlysis bridges the gap between science and implementation by combining physical modelling, simulation, testing logic and practical engineering judgement.

How we work

A practical path from technical uncertainty to engineering decision.

1

Define the real bottleneck

We clarify the technical question, success metric, operating window and current assumptions.

2

Build the right model

We choose the simplest useful calculation, simulation or test logic to answer the engineering question.

3

Validate against reality

We compare assumptions with available data, experimental constraints and material/process limitations.

4

Decide the next move

We deliver clear recommendations: redesign, test plan, simulation roadmap or scale-up decision.
