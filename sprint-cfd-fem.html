Get simulation results that tell you which design direction to pursue — before you build a physical prototype.

What's included

  • Geometry review and simplification strategy for your system
  • Boundary condition identification and validation
  • Simplified model construction (2D or 3D as appropriate)
  • Sensitivity analysis on key design parameters
  • Up to 3 design variants compared side-by-side

Good for you if

  • You need simulation results before committing to a prototype build
  • You want to compare reactor or heat exchanger geometries quickly
  • You have a thermal system with uncertain performance boundaries
  • You need objective data for investor, partner, or board meetings
  • You are evaluating whether CFD/FEM is worth pursuing at full scale
